How Hw3MaMe Services Works

1. Sign Up

Business owners and workers create accounts with basic details and choose their role.

2. Verify & List

Workers complete verification levels to improve their trust score and visibility.

3. Match & Contract

Owners search, filter, and hire workers, then sign digital contracts through the platform.

4. Operate & Track

Workers run the business, while owners monitor performance reports and ratings.
